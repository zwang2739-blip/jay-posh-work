Introduction

Based in Taipei, Taiwan

Driven by a profound love for cinema and music, I believe visuals are not just static images, but a language of emotion. I don't just design; I orchestrate narratives. My approach combines the precision of graphic design with the raw emotion of photography to create high-end, textured visuals that elevate brand value.

深受電影美術與音樂的啟發，我相信影像不僅是視覺的呈現，更是情感的語言。我不僅僅是在做設計，而是在編織故事。我的創作核心在於結合平面設計的精準度與攝影的原始張力，為品牌創造出具備「高級感」與「故事性」的視覺體驗。
